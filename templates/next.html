How to participate

  1. Prepare & upload your budget CSV

    To correctly map the data, we need an ‘Amount’ data column, free of commas and spaces. Get started here.

  2. Create and map a Fiscal Data Package

    Use our new tools to create a Fiscal Data Package and map the data from your CSV file.

  3. Found an issue or bug?

    Your feedback goes a long way towards improving our applications. Please report any issues here.

  4. Suggest an improvement

    Submit suggestions here. Please describe your improvement, giving as much context as possible.
